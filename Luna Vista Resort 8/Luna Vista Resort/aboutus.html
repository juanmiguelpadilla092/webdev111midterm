Our Story

Luna Vista Resort is an always-on sanctuary for travelers seeking the perfect balance of adventure and tranquility. We create innovative, beautiful experiences that empower our guests to find more moments of peace. The resort started in 2021 as a side project between three friends with a passion for coastal architecture. Today, Luna Vista serves thousands of visitors from around the world, yet remains 100% committed to the original vision of sustainable, high-end hospitality.
